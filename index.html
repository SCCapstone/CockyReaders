What is CockyReaders?

Put simply, it's an e-reader for kids.

Personalized Accounts

Every student can have his/her own account. Everything is totally personalized.

Simple to Use

No extra elements or confusing note-taking abilities - CockyReaders gets straight to the point. Your bookshelf is with you wherever you go and your place in each book is remembered.

Backend Magic

Behind the scenes, the app keeps track of all kinds of reading data including time spent reading, number of pages read, progress on each book, etc.. This data is all kept in a secure database for teachers and administrators to analyze.

How?

How do you use the CockyReaders app?

  • Register for an account

    This can either be done on the app or via the Administrator View. This way, a student can register for an account on an iPad or his/her teacher can create an account for him/her and assign specific books to that student.

  • Login via the App

    Download the app on the App Store to your iPad mini. Once you've got it, just login using your username and password (ask your teacher if you're not sure).


  • Start Reading!

    Now you're ready to start reading! Pick a book from your bookshelf and go. Cocky will be with you every step of the way pushing you to read all of the books you've been assigned. Plus, everytime you login in, no matter which iPad you use, your book will open right back up to where you left off.

  • View Your Progress

    By logging into the Administrator View, teacher's can easily track the progress of each student. Not only can teachers see how much time each student has read, but he/she can also see how many pages each student has read, what books are assigned, and even assign new books.
